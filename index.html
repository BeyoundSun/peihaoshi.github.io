The rational is actual and the actual is rational. — G.W.F. Hegel

I hold a Ph.D. in Economics from Wuhan University, with my primary research focusing on enterprise economy and industrial economy.

My work has been published in several leading economics journals, including Technological Forecasting and Social Change, Business Strategy and the Environment, Corporate Social Responsibility and Environmental Management, and Economic Analysis and Policy. I have participated in two research projects funded by the National Social Science Fund of China and authored two monographs. Several of my articles have been republished in other academic journals. I have been awarded the Yugang-Songxiao Scholarship and recognized as an Outstanding Graduate by the university.

I possess strong technical research skills, with proficiency in Stata, R, Python, MATLAB, and EndNote. I also have an advanced level of English proficiency.
